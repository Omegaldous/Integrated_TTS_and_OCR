Integrated TTS and OCR Tutorial Page

Our Integrated TTS and OCR app offers various features for those in need. Come and let's check it out!

Integrated TTS and OCR provides support for different languages, whether locally or globally.

For local use, you can choose between Tagalog, Ilocano, Cebuano, and Ilonggo.

For global use, you can also choose between English, Chinese, Japanese, and Korean.

This app includes OCR, also known as "Optical Character Recognition," a technology that extracts text from images or scanned documents. It employs computer vision, pattern recognition, and machine learning techniques to recognize and interpret characters within an image.
